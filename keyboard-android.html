LigaCheh for Android

The LSDKeyboard input method for Android.

An Android input method for Lisān ud-Daʿwat with the same layout family as the iOS keyboard. It’s now in closed beta on Google Play, and the source is also available.

Join the closed beta on Google Play Source on GitHub

Like the rest of LigaCheh, the Android keyboard stores nothing off your device — see the Privacy Policy.
