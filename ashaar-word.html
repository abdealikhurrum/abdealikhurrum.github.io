Microsoft Word add-in

ashaar.js for Word

Type and justify Arabic-script poetry inside Word.

A Word task-pane add-in for entering Arabic, Urdu, and Persian poetry and laying it out as native right-to-left tables — with professional kashida justification powered by ashaar.js.

View more details on the the Ashaar.JS add-in project page..

Features

  • Two workflows: draw blank poem grids and type into them, or paste marked-up poetry and convert it.
  • Inserts poetry as native Word RTL tables with layout presets (balanced, equal, compact, stacked).
  • One-click kashida justification that balances each misra to the longest line, in place.
  • Wraps each poem in a tagged Word content control so layout and source are preserved.
