LigaCheh Keyboard · iOS & iPadOS

LigaCheh for iPhone & iPad

A keyboard built for the script, with smart suggestions.

LigaCheh is a custom keyboard for Lisān ud-Daʿwat with three layouts (LSD, standard Arabic, and CRULP Urdu), a full diacritics layer, double-press shortcuts for extended letters, and on-device next-word suggestions. A companion app adds a notepad and guided lessons.

Highlights

  • Three layouts — LSD, Arabic, and Urdu — switchable in a tap.
  • Double-tap a key for its secondary letter (ٹ, پ, ھ, چ, …).
  • A full tashkeel (diacritics) layer with cursor control.
  • On-device suggestions; right-to-left aware, with a mixed Latin/Arabic fixer.
  • Everything stays on your device — no tracking, no accounts.
